Spaghetti Carbonara

25 mins Main Dish Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated cheeses and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water, and immediately add to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese and pepper.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

35 mins Bakery Makes 24

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 170g unsalted butter, melted
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 100g white sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 300g chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 165°C (325°F). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Sift together flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. Cream together melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until well blended.
  4. Beat in vanilla, egg, and egg yolk until light and creamy.
  5. Mix in sifted ingredients until just blended.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips by hand.
  7. Drop cookie dough by tablespoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 15-17 minutes or until edges are lightly toasted.
  9. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks.
